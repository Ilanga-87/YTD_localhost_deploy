How to get link from YouTube in browser

The easiest way is just to copy it from the address bar in your browser. Select all text in the string, then press Ctrl+C (or right mouse button and select "Copy" in the list). After this you can put your cursor into field in our form and press Ctrl+V (or right mouse button and select "Paste" in the list.). Then just press the SEND button

Also you can find a button SHARE below the video. After this you will see a little pop-up window where you can just press COPY. And paste the link in our form as described above. Of course, do not forget to press SEND button

How to get link from YouTube in mobile app

In your YouTube mobile app there is a row of icons behind the video description. Select SHARE icon and press it. After that you will see COPY icon in left top corner. Press it and you will get your link. Now just put it in the first form field with long pressing and PASTE option. Then press SEND button
